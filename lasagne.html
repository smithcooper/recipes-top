Lasagne

Description

Everybody knows Lasagne, and everybody thinks that their recipie is best. Only one of those people are right and I can't promise that it is me. I can promise you a warm lasagne, full of love and flavour for you to enjoy.

Serve this dish with your favourite sides; mine are chips and a greek salad. This makes for a wonderful dinner that everyone will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet refridgerated pasta sheets
  • 1 large onion
  • 1 large carrot
  • 10cm celery stalk
  • 3-6 garlic cloves
  • Olive Oil
  • 1200g canned cherry tomatoes (peeled or chopped)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese (Optional: Cheddar, Tasty, Parmesan, Peccorino, Mozzarella)
  • Beef or pork mince (Optional: substitute additional onions, carrots and celary)
