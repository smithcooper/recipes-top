Penne Arrabiata

Description

This is a classic Italian pasta dish. Combining a rich tomato sauce with spicy chilli. If you like it hot add more chillis and include the seeds! To make it more mild, ensure that you remove the seeds and membrane from the chilli.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large onion diced finely
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1-5 small red chillies chopped
  • 800g can diced tomatoes
  • 125ml vegetable stock
  • Bunch of basil leaves
  • 500g Penne
  • Pinch of sugar
