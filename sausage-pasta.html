Sausage Pasta

Description

Sausage Pasta is the perfect dish for any leftovers you might have in your fridge. I cook this once every few weeks to eliminate food waste and to make sure I have leftovers for lunch!

This recipe is a guide, but you can add anything to this to make it your own.

Ingredients

  • 500g pasta
  • Olive oil
  • 500g flavoured sausage (meat or vegan will work)
  • 1 capsicum cut lengthways
  • A handful of cherry tomatoes
  • Baby spinach
  • 1 Large onion diced
  • 4 cloves garlic finely diced

Steps

  1. Bring heavily salted pasta water to the boil.
  2. Heat olive oil over a medium heat. Add onion and garlic and cook until onion softens.
  3. Cook sausage and break apart with a wooden spoon.
  4. Add tomatoes and capsicum and reduce heat. Meanwhile cook the pasta.
  5. When the pasta is finished, drain and combine with the sauce.
  6. Add spinach and stir until wilted.
  7. Serve!
