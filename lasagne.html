Lasagne

Description

Everybody knows Lasagne, and everybody thinks that their recipie is best. Only one of those people are right and I can't promise that it is me. I can promise you a warm lasagne, full of love and flavour for you to enjoy.

Serve this dish with your favourite sides; mine are chips and a greek salad. This makes for a wonderful dinner that everyone will enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1 packet refridgerated pasta sheets
  • 1 large onion diced
  • 1 large carrot diced
  • 10cm celery stalk diced
  • 3-6 garlic cloves finely diced
  • Dry red or white wine
  • 2 bayleaves
  • A few thyme sprigs
  • Olive Oil
  • 1200g canned cherry tomatoes (peeled or chopped)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Cheese (Optional: Cheddar, Tasty, Parmesan, Peccorino, Mozzarella)
  • Beef or pork mince (Optional: substitute additional onions, carrots and celary)

Steps

Making the Sauce

  1. Heat oil in a large pan over a medium heat. I prefer to use a dutch oven for this.
  2. Add onion and sauté for about a minute.
  3. Add carrot and celery and continue to sauté for another minute. Add mince here if you choose.
  4. Add garlic and continue to cook until garlic is cooked, roughly 2 minutes.
  5. Lower the heat to low and add a splash of wine, scrape anything off the bottom of the pan.
  6. Add tomatos to the pan.
  7. Tie up bayleaves and thyme with kitchen twine and put into the sauce.
  8. If you do not have kitchen twine that is okay! Just put the bayleaves in the pot and add the thyme leaves only to the pot.
  9. Cook for as long as possible with the lid on, up to 4 hours, stirring occasionally
  10. Take out bayleaves.

Assembling the Lasagne

  1. Lightly grease the outside of the lasagne tray.
  2. Place a small amount of sauce into the tray, just enough to cover the bottom layer.
  3. Lay a layer of pasta sheets, then a layer of sauce. You can also add cheese over the sauce.
  4. Repeat step three until you are out of pasta sheets or sauce. Remember to finish with a layer of sauce.
  5. Add the rest of the cheese.

Cooking the Lasagne

  1. Bake for about 40 minutes or until the cheese is starting to turn golden.
  2. Take lasagne out of the oven and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  3. Serve!
