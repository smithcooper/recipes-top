Cacio e Pepe

Description

This pasta dish is a staple. Cacio e Pepe literally translates to "Cheese and Pepper" It is sometimes referred to as the mac and cheese for adults and it really is!

Cacio e Pepe is as simple as it gets. It only has a few ingredients, but it is fantastic!

Ingredients

  • 500g quality pasta (Traditionally long pasta, such as spaghetti, is used)
  • 100g unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 150g Pecorino-Romano cheese

Steps

  1. Bring pasta water to the boil and add a handful of salt. The water should be as salty as the sea!
  2. Melt butter in a pan and add the pepper. Cook for 30 seconds and turn off the heat.
  3. Cook the pasta for one minute less than the packet time.
  4. Add pasta to the pan with the pepper. Retain some pasta water.
  5. Add cheese stir until melted. Continue to add small amounts of pasta water to keep a silky consistency.
  6. Serve!
