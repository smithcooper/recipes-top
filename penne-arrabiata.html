Penne Arrabiata

Description

This is a classic Italian pasta dish. Combining a rich tomato sauce with spicy chilli. If you like it hot add more chillis and include the seeds! To make it more mild, ensure that you remove the seeds and membrane from the chilli.

Ingredients

  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 large onion diced finely
  • 2 crushed garlic cloves
  • 1-5 small red chillies chopped
  • 800g can diced tomatoes
  • 125ml vegetable stock
  • Bunch of basil leaves
  • 500g Penne
  • Pinch of sugar

Steps

  1. Bring heavily salted water to boil.
  2. Heat oil in a large pan over a medium heat, adding onion and garlic. Cook for a few minutes until onion starts to soften.
  3. Add tomatoes, stock and about half of the basil leaves. Reduce heat to low and cook for 10-15 minutes uncovered.
  4. Cook pasta.
  5. Drain pasta and return to pot.
  6. Add sugar to the sauce.
  7. Mix sauce through pasta.
  8. Set aside for a few minutes with the lid over the pot.
  9. Serve with the other half of the basil.
